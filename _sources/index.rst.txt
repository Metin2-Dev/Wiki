Welcome to the Metin2Dev Wiki
==============================

If you are new here you might not notice but if you are not
you will notice that the wiki is completely different

If you want to keep in pair with the changes in real time
check out the
`development/beta site <https://metin2-dev.github.io/wiki-beta-dev/>`_,
and also the
`development/beta site repository <https://github.com/Metin2-Dev/wiki-beta-dev>`_
if you are interested in discussions and details about progress

You can still visit the old wiki at the usual place in
https://metin2.dev/wiki while we are setting all up
for this new one

We are in process of building a whole new wiki and other
cool things, so we thank you for your patience while waiting

Any news or progress will be announced here, on the
`Forum <https://metin2.dev/>`_ or on
`Discord <https://discord.gg/metin2dev>`_, so stay tuned!
